2. Nature of Our Services

HEAVEN NIGHTS LLP operates in the travel and tourism industry as a Business-to-Business (B2B) destination management company, providing travel support services to travel agencies, including accommodation arrangements, transportation coordination, tour activities, and related guest support services.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and business information from our partner agencies and guest travelers.

A. Partner Agency Information

We may collect:

  • Business name
  • Business registration details
  • Business address
  • Contact person details
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Billing and payment information
  • Communication records

B. Guest Traveler Information

We may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Nationality
  • Aadhaar or other identity proof where required
  • Flight details
  • Hotel preferences
  • Emergency contact information
  • Child age information
  • Medical or dietary information where operationally necessary

Children’s information may be collected where necessary for travel arrangements, accommodation allocation, pricing, or related operational purposes.

4. How Information Is Collected

We collect information through:

  • Phone calls
  • Email communications
  • Registration forms
  • WhatsApp communications
  • Business correspondence
  • Social media communication handled by our marketing team

5. Purpose of Collection

We collect and process information for the following purposes:

  • Providing travel management services
  • Booking accommodations
  • Coordinating transportation
  • Managing guest activities
  • Customer support
  • Business communication
  • Billing, accounting, and financial compliance
  • Operational coordination with vendors and service providers

6. Sharing of Information

We may share relevant guest information strictly on a need-to-know basis with:

  • Hotels
  • Transportation providers
  • Drivers
  • Tour guides

Shared information is limited to operationally necessary details such as guest names, nationality, contact details, and group information.

Partner agency information may be shared internally and with authorized accounting professionals, auditors, or tax consultants where required for financial, accounting, or legal compliance.

We do not sell personal information.

7. Payments and Financial Transactions

We process business payments through:

  • UPI

  • Other lawful payment methods

Payments may involve coordination with hotels, transportation vendors, and other travel service providers.

8. Data Retention
